Volkswagen Factory Closure Analysis

Option 4: Close Zwickau, Hanover, and Emden

Factory Annual Savings (€) Production Loss (Units)
Zwickau €740,000,000 325,000 cars
Hanover €540,000,000 175,000 cars
Emden €640,000,000 200,000 cars

Summary of Factory Closures:

Total Savings: €1,920,000,000 annually

Total Production Loss: 700,000 cars per year (325,000 + 175,000 + 200,000)

Result: This option nearly meets the €2 billion savings target, but it would have a significant impact on production. The company will need to assess the long-term effects on market share, supply chain, and overall production capabilities.
